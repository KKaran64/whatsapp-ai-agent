## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Is cork durable?**
A: Yes! Cork is extremely durable. It's used in wine bottles for centuries. Water-resistant, doesn't crack, and lasts years with proper care.

**Q: Can cork get wet?**
A: Cork is naturally water-resistant. While it can absorb some moisture, it dries quickly and doesn't get damaged. Perfect for coasters!

**Q: How to clean cork products?**
A: Wipe with damp cloth. For tough stains, use mild soap. Avoid soaking. Air dry.

**Q: Can you add our company logo?**
A: Yes! We offer engraving/printing for bulk orders (50+ pieces). ₹20-30 per piece depending on complexity.

**Q: What's the minimum order?**
A: No minimum for retail. For customization and bulk pricing, minimum is 50-100 pieces depending on product.

**Q: Delivery time?**
A: Standard orders: 7-10 days. Bulk/custom orders: 15-20 days.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Yes, on request. Contact us for international shipping quote.

**Q: Return policy?**
A: 7-day return for defective products. Custom orders are non-returnable.
